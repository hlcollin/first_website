About Me!

My name is Heather Collins.

I am a student at Cal Poly SLO studying Graphic Communication. I hope to one day establish a career in the area of design, print, or web. I enjoy dance, running, cutting up old magazines, reading obscure books, and the ocean.

I love California. I was born in its rugged Sierra Nevada foothills in what used to be Gold Country. I moved 600 miles south to Orange County at the end of my childhood. There I attended a community college before transferring to Cal Poly and the Central Coast. The places I have lived serve as constant inspiration to me. I hope to travel extensively and add to this collection of inspiration.

This website serves as a historical archive of my first struggles with HTML and CSS. I look forward to learning more in this area and one day look back at this site with amusement, in the same way I look back at old elementary school art projects.
